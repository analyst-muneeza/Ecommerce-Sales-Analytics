# Power BI Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard was developed to analyze E-Commerce sales performance, customer behavior, product performance, and delivery efficiency.

The dashboard provides a complete business view through five analytical pages.

## Dashboard Pages

1. Executive Dashboard
2. Sales Performance
3. Product Analysis
4. Customer Analysis
5. Business Summary

---

## Key Business Insights

- Revenue is primarily driven by a few high-performing product categories.
- The Southeast region contributes the largest customer base and overall sales.
- Best-selling categories generate most of the revenue, while premium products have lower order volumes.
- 92.13% of orders were delivered on time, reflecting efficient delivery performance.
- Monthly order trends show noticeable fluctuations across the year.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Increase marketing efforts in high-performing regions.
- Maintain sufficient inventory for best-selling product categories.
- Improve sales strategies in lower-performing regions.
- Continue optimizing delivery operations to maintain customer satisfaction.
- Monitor monthly order trends to support demand and inventory planning.
